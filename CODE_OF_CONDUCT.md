# Code of Conduct

We want Breakfast Club contributions to be open, respectful, and useful.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on accurate, up-to-date information.
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Repeatedly submitting knowingly false/stale data.
- Doxxing or sharing private personal information.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove or edit contributions that violate this code and may limit contributor access for repeated abuse.

## Scope
This applies to issues, pull requests, and repo discussions.
